General

Where do you operate from?

ALTA labs is formed of a global team of developers, project managers, and engineers. In a way, we’re everywhere. Legally speaking, our business has a physical headquarters in Birmingham, Alabama.

How quickly can you scale our team?

This depends on a few factors. First is your needs. We’ll go basically as quickly as you want to go, with a couple other factors in mind. Then, we must consider the amount of planning, design, and research necessary for your project or business. Lastly, of course, is your budget. To get answers to these questions, it’s best to start with an obligation-free consult and explore your goals and objectives from there.

Do you only work with certain types of software?

In a word, nope! We’ll build your perfect dev team around your tech stack. It doesn't matter whether you use a licensed or open source solution; if it connects to the internet, we’ll be able to work with it. 
PS: If your system doesn’t connect to the internet, we have a physical systems integrator who might be able to set you up with a new system that does

How does billing work?

We bill weekly or bi-weekly automatically, according to the spend limits you place on your account. We find its helpful to separate ongoing costs like support from special projects, and we can help you manage that. If you desire a turnkey setup, we may be able to negotiate that.

all_inbox

Capabilities & Support

Can you provide a support SLA?

In a word, yes. Whether you need a fifteen minute or a fifteen hour turnaround, we can tailor something to your needs. Plus, ongoing support is already a part of our process.

I already have an internal development or IT team, can you work together?

We’ll work with your existing team as we explore your current tech and provide an evaluation. From there, we can tailor our offering to either augment or complement your existing team, or to operate independently. In other words, it’s up to you.

If I hire a team, what does it look like?

The first person you’ll meet is your Success Manager. They make sure you and your business get the results you specify. We’ll also build you the perfect dev team. This could consist of a combination of engineers and software developers depending on your specific needs—i.e. backend versus front end, or sales versus fulfillment. If you have a project on the larger side, you’ll also meet a project manager who spearheads the work and ensures it gets completed in a timely manner.

Can you work with software x?

We actually only work with Neuralink. Jokes. Yes, we’ll work with anything. If you’re using some specialized software solution, we’ll find the right expert to navigate it. We have access to multiple global networks of developers with an exceptionally wide range of skills.
